DimensionAnalysis
Confirmed FactsHHH framework published Apr 2022 as research paper. Constitutional AI (May 2023) resolved the helpful-vs-harmless trade-off. RSP v1.0 through v3.0 created escalating safety levels. Claude's Constitution (Jan 2026) made values public under CC0 license. Two red lines (autonomous weapons, mass surveillance) maintained through federal ban.
Direct QuotesDA: "You can have a perfectly harmless model today — it just wouldn't be very helpful. We're not gonna move on those red lines."
Leverage PointsHHH framing creates market differentiation (safety as competitive advantage). Constitutional AI makes safety scalable. Public values document (CC0) makes retreat from stated positions reputationally costly.
Unresolved Tensions'Helpful' in defense context means aiding military operations. 'Harmless' has different meaning when the user is a weapons system. 'Honest' requires transparency about what Claude is being used for — the Venezuela/Maduro inquiry suggests Anthropic may not always know.
Evidenced vs SpeculativeEVIDENCED: HHH framework exists, is published, is integrated into training. Red lines were held under maximum pressure. SPECULATIVE: Whether HHH framework can survive indefinitely as commercial and defense pressures scale.
